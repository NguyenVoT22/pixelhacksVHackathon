STOP THE HATE!

Hate crimes happen all across the United States, and they happen more often than we know. But how come we DON'T know? That is because a majority of them go unreported.

STOP THE HATE's aim is to get people feel safer about reporting hate crimes that they witnessed or are victims of, and to provide resources to help people come together to STOP THE HATE!

Hate crimes are still happening across the U.S.
You deserve to live in a world free of hate.
